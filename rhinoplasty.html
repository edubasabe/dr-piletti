Rhinoplasty

Rhinoplasty also known as Nose Job is a surgery to
improve not only the sahpe but the function of your nose.

GENERAL RHINOPLASTY

This surgery in experienced hands is a very simple procedure that will bring you great satisfaction.

The nasal anatomy has the same structural elements for each nose but different in size and layout, so its great variety in shapes and appearances. Nasal surgery is to restructure the arrangement and proportion of these structural elements for the nose look harmonious with the rest of the face.

If it's a big nose all the visual attention will be on it but if it's very small goes unnoticed.
For Dr. Piletti is important to analyze all facial structures and seek greater harmony between them. Ears, chin, cheeks, cheekbones and lips should be in balance for the best aesthetic balance of his face.

TYPES OF RHINOPLASTY

There are many types of rhinoplasty, depending on the wound to be done to address their anatomy will be open rhinoplasty (a small wound on the columella always go unnoticed) or closed rhinoplasty through two small cuts inside the nostrils.

Depending on the shape and size there is an Augmentation or reduction rhinoplasty. It will be a primary rhinoplasty if not previously operated and secondary or Revision Rhinoplasty if is already Operate and want to correct the result.

Dr Piletti will choose the procedure that fits your case and use the most appropriate technique to achieve the expected result for you type.

ETHNIC RHINOPLASTY

The African Americans, Asian or mestizo nose patients (Mexican and Central American) rhinoplasty is called ethnic and uniformly requires:

  • Increase or remodeling of the nasal dorsum
  • Definition of the nasal tip
  • Reducing the width of the nostrils

The nasal dorsum must have an adequate height to visually separate the cheeks, the back is low when we corrected using a cartilage graft from the patient himself. Dr. Piletti handled nasal master technique increase with costal cartilage unlike nasal silicone implants are not rejected or has complications.

The nasal tip in African American and Asian patients has a rounded shape so you have to rearrange their structure to give a geometrically defined shape and project it properly. The nasal width such noses is corrected by reducing the size of the nostril or nostrils which is another part of the desired balance.

Each of these points is crucial. in particular the first two require the use an implant or a graft. Our preference is to use the patient's own cartilage as it is a more artistic and more natural results on surgery.

The sculpted cartilage graft from the patient do a personalized way. Besides the advantage of used cartilaginous tissue from the same patient so as not to run the risk of complications prefabricated implants are as rejection, infection, skin ulceration and exposure of implant malposition. A nasal implant whatever their origin never will replace the tissue from the same patient.

Our experience is wide more than 2,500 patients have undergone this procedure a complete success, for your complete satisfaction. Dr piletti has extensive experience performing all types of rhinoplasty with thousands of satisfied patients.

SECONDARY RHINOPLASTY

It is correct the unsatisfactory outcome of a previous nasal surgery. This is a fascinating surgery from the professional point of view as most of the time I rebuild the delicate nasal anatomy.

To this end, Dr Piletti with more than 2,000 cases of nasal reconstruction using most often costal cartilage which obtains sufficient autologous material (from the patient) to achieve a perfect result.

Occasionally according patient preference may use nasal prosthesis polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE ) having a high degree of biocompatibility.
